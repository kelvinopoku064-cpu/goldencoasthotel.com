Golden Coast Hotel

Our Story

Golden Coast Hotel is a boutique luxury retreat in the heart of Spintex-Road opposite the Zenith bank, Accra. We blend modern comfort with warm Ghanaian hospitality, offering a peaceful escape for business travelers, tourists, and locals alike.

Our name reflects the harmony between contemporary design and the vibrant energy of Accra — a place where guests feel truly at home.
